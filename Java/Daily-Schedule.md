### Personalized Daily Routine (Early Bird, Office 10:00 AM)

This schedule aligns with the 14-week Roadmap and your early-morning preference. Two weekday variants are provided based on return time.

---

### Weekdays – When Back by 6:00 PM
- 05:30 – 05:40  Wake up, hydrate, light stretch
- 05:40 – 06:00  Morning Exercise (walk/jog/yoga/mobility)
- 06:00 – 06:10  Plan day: pick Core topic, DSA set, Project task (Top 3)
- 06:10 – 07:20  Deep Work 1 (Core Java/Spring) – study + 2 drills
- 07:20 – 07:30  Break (no phone)
- 07:30 – 08:20  Deep Work 2 (DSA) – 2–3 LeetCode mediums, 1 review
- 08:20 – 09:00  Breakfast + get ready
- 09:00 – 09:45  Commute/Pre-work: flashcards or theory notes
- 10:00 – 18:00  Office (ship at least one small task)
- 18:00 – 18:45  Commute/Decompress (music/podcast/walk)
- 18:45 – 19:15  Dinner
- 19:15 – 20:15  Deep Work 3 (Project) – 1 small issue/MR + 1 test
- 20:15 – 20:30  Break
- 20:30 – 21:00  Review/Flashcards (10) + update tracker
- 21:00 – 21:15  Plan tomorrow’s Top 3; desk/IDE ready
- 21:15 – 22:00  Wind down; sleep target ~22:00–22:15

### Weekdays – When Back by 7:00 PM
- 05:30 – 05:40  Wake up, hydrate, light stretch
- 05:40 – 06:00  Morning Exercise (walk/jog/yoga/mobility)
- 06:00 – 06:10  Plan day: Top 3
- 06:10 – 07:30  Deep Work 1 (Core Java/Spring) – longer block
- 07:30 – 07:45  Break
- 07:45 – 08:25  Deep Work 2 (DSA) – 2 mediums
- 08:25 – 09:00  Breakfast + get ready
- 09:00 – 09:45  Commute: flashcards/theory
- 10:00 – 19:00  Office
- 19:00 – 19:45  Commute + Dinner (quick)
- 19:45 – 20:30  Light Block (DSA review or notes tidy-up)
- 20:30 – 21:00  Flashcards (10) + tracker update
- 21:00 – 21:15  Plan tomorrow’s Top 3; prep desk/IDE

Notes:
- If you’re tired after 7 PM, skip the Light Block; keep only flashcards + plan.
- Protect morning deep work by going screen-free 30 mins before sleep.

### Daily Content Mapping (What to do in each block)
- Core Block (AM): topic from Roadmap week; create 3 flashcards; 2 short code drills
- DSA Block (AM): 2–3 mediums of same pattern; 1 review of past mistake; update “patterns notebook”
- Project Block (PM/Weekend): 1 small issue/MR + 1 test; keep PRs small; update README/ADR if architecture changes
- Admin/Review: log progress in tracker; plan Top 3 for tomorrow; clear desk; IDE at next task file

---

### Guardrails
- Focus mode during deep work (DND, phone away, one-tab rule)
- If stuck >10 min: write hypotheses, try 2 approaches, then ask/park
- If schedule slips: keep the morning blocks intact; compress evening

---

### Quick Weekly Targets (for alignment)
- 10–15 LeetCode mediums; 1 timed set on weekend
- 3–5 project commits (tests included)
- 1 design note (HLD/LLD/ADR)
- 5 days of flashcards; 1 weekly mini-mock
